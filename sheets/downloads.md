## Printing Notes

- Print at **100% scale** ("actual size"), not "fit to page."
- The browser versions print identically to the PDFs — open one, press print, done. Regenerate a PDF any time with a browser's *Save as PDF*.
- Nothing on any sheet is mandatory bookkeeping. If your table tracks the faction on a whiteboard and characters on index cards, the game does not mind.
