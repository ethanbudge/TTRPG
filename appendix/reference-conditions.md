# Quick Reference — Conditions

*The status-effect system on one page. The rule: a condition imposes **1 snag** on any contest it would plausibly hinder, and nothing on contests it wouldn't. Hard conditions also **forbid** a category of action, and always land with a named exit.*

## Common Conditions

| Condition | Kind | Typical duration | Typical exit |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| *Off-balance, Dazzled, Winded* | ordinary | momentary | end of the exchange |
| *Shaken, Hesitant, Rattled* | ordinary | conflict | a rally, a breather, a proof of safety |
| *Exposed, Out of Position* | ordinary | conflict | a goal spent repositioning |
| *On Fire, Flooding, Fouled* | ordinary | conflict | a goal spent dealing with it — it worsens if ignored |
| *Hunted, Watched* | ordinary | lasting | lose them, or remove the watcher |
| *Discredited, Out of Favor* | ordinary | lasting | downtime, or a public win |
| *Wounded Pride, Grieving* | ordinary | lasting | downtime, or the story healing it |
| **Pinned / Grappled / Tractor-locked** | hard — can't change bands | conflict | win a Physical contest, or an ally frees you |
| **Blinded / Sensors Dark** | hard — can't act beyond Contact unaided | conflict | aid, time, or remove the cause |
| **Paralyzed / Dead in the Water** | hard — no physical action | conflict | outside help, or the stated end |
| **Silenced / Jammed** | hard — no communication beyond Contact | conflict | remove the cause |
| **Routed / Panicked** | hard — can't act toward the conflict's goal | conflict | a rally by you or an ally |

## Faction-Scale Conditions

| Condition | What it snags | Typical exit |
| --- | --- | --- |
| *Starving, Falling Apart, Restless, Doubting* | contests and income tied to the starved need | pay the [Keep](faction/resources.md#going-hungry) |
| *Compromised* | anything needing secrecy | find the leak (Spy, or a mission) |
| *Discredited (regional)* | influence and diplomacy in that region | Campaign actions, public wins |
| *Overextended* | reacting to new threats | consolidate — abandon or reinforce |
| *Headless* | coordinated faction action | crown a new Stronghold |
| *Oathbreaker* | being trusted, ever | long, public penance |

## Rulings in Five Seconds

- Does the condition plausibly hinder this contest? Snag. Would it not? No snag. The table decides in one breath.
- Hard conditions still snag whatever else they'd plausibly hinder.
- Conditions on the winner's side of a contest don't transfer; each actor carries their own.
- Stacking duplicates does nothing — a second *Shaken* sharpens the story, not the snag.
- Scars are conditions promoted to [flaws](characters/traits.md#scars-are-flaws): permanent, personal, and part of the character now.
