# Quick Reference — The Faction Turn

*The machine on one page. Runs whenever the GM lets real time pass. Twenty to thirty minutes, played together, steps dealt around the table.*

## The Four Steps

**1 · Income** — each holding produces per its Gives line; doubled Seats add their synergy stock. Hungry lines produce 1 less. Note where everything lands.

**2 · Keep** — pay the faction's needs from stock that can reach them. Unpaid: a matching condition lands, and repeated misses run a countdown toward real loss ([Going Hungry](faction/resources.md#going-hungry)). Everything paid with spare: **Flourishing** — one income line +1, or defense with an edge.
*Time also moves the map here: Remote → Reachable → Ready, unless Cut Off.*

**3 · Actions** — one per player, +1 per Head (max +2). Led by a player, rolled as a contest on a faction stat. Committed stock is spent win or lose.

| Action | Stat | Effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Put Down Roots | varies | Start/strike a new-holding countdown (4) |
| Develop | Phys/Men | Build an asset, project countdown, improve a holding |
| Campaign | Emo/Ideo | Strike agenda countdowns, pin conditions (burns 1 Influence/Zeal) |
| Strike | Phys | Hit a rival holding, cache, or asset (burns 1 Force) |
| Spy / Sabotage | Men | Steal intel, cut stock off, pin conditions quietly |
| Trade | Emo/Men | Swap 2:1 at market, 1:1 with Allies; move stock the long way |
| Quell / Tend | Emo/Ideo | Clear faction conditions, restore 1 Cohesion |
| Recruit | Ideo | +1 Zeal or Force stock (2 on a Triumph) |

**4 · Downtime** — each character: **Recover** (clear your lasting conditions) · **Treat** (clear another's; or quiet a scar-flaw for the arc) · **Prepare** (prime an edge for the next job) · **Work** (+1 stock where you are) · **Scheme** (personal countdown) · **Tend the Flame** (restore 1 Cohesion, or clear a people's condition here).

**Then rivals move** — one action each toward their visible countdowns; two for major rivals at tables of six-plus.

## Accessibility

**Ready** — commit it now · **Reachable** — one goal or an hour fetches it · **Remote** — not this conflict; time brings it closer · **Cut Off** — blocked until dealt with in play.

## Cohesion

Starts at 5, drains on: a holding lost (−1; Stronghold −2) · public humiliation of the cause (−1) · internal schism (−1). Restore via Quell/Tend and Tend the Flame. **At 0: Crisis** — play it out; survivors rebuild to 2.

## Spending Stock

Faction Resource edge: 1 Ready stock, max 1 per contest · Actions: as listed · Asset: 2 matching stock + an action (or 3 perks) · Keep shortfall: cover internal needs with same-stat external stock at 2:1.
