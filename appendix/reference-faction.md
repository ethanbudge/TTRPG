# Quick Reference — The Faction Turn

*The machine on one page. Runs whenever the GM lets real time pass. Twenty to thirty minutes, played together, steps dealt around the table.*

## The Three Steps

**1 · Income** — each holding produces per its Gives line; doubled Seats add their synergy stock. Hungry lines produce 1 less. Note where everything lands.

**2 · Keep** — settle the faction's needs. **Consumed needs** are spent each turn (rations, wages); **standing needs** must merely be present (a garrison's loyalty), failing only when that stock leaves. Unmet: a matching condition lands, then a countdown toward real loss ([Going Hungry](faction/resources.md#going-hungry)). Everything met with spare: **Flourishing** — one income line +1, or defense with an edge.
*Time also moves the map here: Remote → Reachable → Ready, unless Cut Off.*

**3 · Do Something** — each player takes **one move**: a faction action *or* a personal move. Each Head takes one extra **faction action** on top, personally.

| Faction action | Stat | Effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Put Down Roots | varies | Countdown to establish something lasting — cache (2), safehouse (4), holding (6), region (8) |
| Develop | Phys/Men | Build an asset, project countdown, research, fortify, improve a holding |
| Campaign | Emo/Ideo | Strike agenda countdowns, pin conditions (burns 1 Influence/Zeal) |
| Strike | Phys | Hit a rival holding, cache, or asset (burns 1 Force) |
| Spy / Sabotage | Men | Steal intel, cut stock off, pin conditions quietly |
| Trade | Emo | Swap stock 1:1 with a faction at Friendly or better |
| Quell / Tend | Emo/Ideo | Clear faction conditions, restore 1 Cohesion |
| Recruit | Ideo | +1 Zeal or Force stock (2 on a Triumph) |

| Personal move | Effect |
| --- | --- |
| Prepare | Bank a primed edge for the next job |
| Recover | Clear your own lasting conditions |
| Pursue | Start or strike a personal countdown |
| Reach Out | Mend a bond, call in a favor, deepen a tie |

**Then rivals move** — one action each toward their visible countdowns; two for major rivals at tables of six-plus.

## Accessibility

**Ready** — commit it now · **Reachable** — one goal or an hour fetches it · **Remote** — not this conflict; time brings it closer · **Cut Off** — blocked until dealt with in play.

## Cohesion

Starts at 5, drains on: a holding lost (−1; Stronghold −2) · public humiliation of the cause (−1) · internal schism (−1). Restore via Quell/Tend. **At 0: Crisis** — play it out; survivors rebuild to 2.

## Spending Stock

Faction Resource edge: 1 Ready stock, max 1 per contest · Actions: as listed · Asset: 2 matching stock + an action (or 3 perks) · Keep shortfall: cover internal needs with same-stat external stock at 2:1.
