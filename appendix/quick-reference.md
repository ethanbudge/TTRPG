# Quick Reference

*Everything at the table, one page.*

## The Contest

1. **Goal** — one sentence: what do you want to be true?
2. **Stakes** — GM says what a loss means, before dice.
3. **Stat** — your approach picks it; the rating is your pool.
4. **Edges/setbacks** — ±1 die each. Sources (max one each): Craft (feature/gift) · Aid (one ally) · Position · Resources (1 stock = 1 edge, max 2). Clamp pool 1–5; overflow strips the other side's dice.
5. **Roll & compare sums.**

| Outcome | Effect |
| --- | --- |
| Win | Goal happens |
| Win by 5+ | Goal + one boon |
| **Tie** | **Acting side succeeds, at a cost** |
| Lose | Stakes land |
| Lose by 5+ | Stakes land hard |

**Odds:** 3v3 ≈ 55% · 4v3 ≈ 81% · 5v3 ≈ 94% · 5v2 ≈ 99%.

**Perks in play (1/contest):** 1 perk = reroll one die, or Prepare (pre-roll Position edge).

## Harm

- Lose a harmful contest: **mark 1 strain** (2 if by 5+). Track = 5 boxes (mooks 1–2, assets/squads 3).
- **Protection tag:** negate 1 strain, once per scene each.
- Track full = **taken out** (winner narrates within stakes) — or accept a **scar** (permanent condition, +1 perk, clear 2).
- Strain clears with a real breather. Conditions persist by tier: momentary / scene / lasting / scar.
- **Condition:** −1 die when it plausibly applies. **Hard conditions** forbid an action type; always land with a named exit.

## Conflict

- GM sets **clockspeed**: Blitz (seconds) / Operation (hours) / Season (weeks) / Era (months).
- Each beat: every actor declares one goal, resolves one contest. No initiative — fiction picks order.
- **Bands:** Contact / Close / Far / Distant. 1 band free with your goal; 2+ = the goal. Range tags are permissions.
- **Set-up:** any win can bank an edge for a named ally instead of effect.
- **Scale tiers:** Personal / Company / Host. Up a tier: no track marks without leverage — wins make edges, ticks, conditions. Down a tier: wins mark 2, but the small evade with an edge.
- **Faction conflict adds:** **Channel** (roll faction stat/asset rating) · **Commit** (stock → edges, must be reachable) · **Deploy** (bring asset, full goal) · **Marshal** (clear/move/rally units).

## Faction Turn (20–30 min)

1. **Yield** — nodes produce.
2. **Upkeep** — nodes eat; shortfall → condition → strain → flip. All needs met + 2 spare = *Flourishing*.
3. **Logistics** — move stock/assets along routes (safe free, threatened contested, cut closed).
4. **Actions** — one per player (+1/Head, max +2): Expand · Fortify/Develop · Campaign · Strike · Spy/Sabotage · Trade · Quell/Tend · Recruit.
5. **Downtime** — each PC: Recover · Treat · Prepare · Work · Scheme · Tend the Flame. Spend perks.

Then rivals each take one action; their clocks tick.

## Perks

**Earn:** flaw bites (+1, max 1/flaw/session) · scar (+1) · session end: showed up +1, agenda advanced +1, personal ambition +1.
**Spend:** reroll/Prepare 1 · class feature 4 · cross-class 5 · gift 4 · stat +1 8 — or faction: route 2 · asset 3 · node upgrade 3 · specialist 4 · new node 5 · faction stat +1 8 (poolable). Every 3rd advance: free **Deepen**.

## Opposition Ladder

Trivial 1 · Weak 2 · Standard 3 · Hard 4 · Formidable 5 — announced openly, always.
