# The Mind

*Everyone else asks whether it can be done. The faction keeps you around so somebody asks HOW.*

The Mind is the office of **expertise and the impossible**: the engineer, wizard, surgeon, forger, alchemist, ritualist, or systems operator. Whatever your genre calls its miracle-workers, the Mind is where the setting's rules bend. This is also the natural home for supernatural power in campaigns that have it — see [Open Questions](appendix/open-questions.md) on a fuller magic module.

## Core Feature — Of Course I Can

Once per session, declare a feat of expertise the table agrees is *possible for your discipline but absurd under pressure* — recalibrating the drive mid-burn, open-heart surgery in a moving wagon, forging the treaty overnight. The GM must allow the goal to be attempted, and you roll with an edge.

## Faction Synergy

**Develop** actions (research, construction, innovation) gain an edge when a Mind helps run them. If the faction has more than one Mind, it also gains +1 internal Mental stock (*Expertise*) at the Yield step.

## Class Features (start with two)

| Feature | Effect |
| --- | --- |
| **Jury-Rig** | Once per scene, assemble a working tool, device, or ritual from materials at hand. It functions for one scene, then fails spectacularly. |
| **Blueprint Mind** | After spending a scene inside any structure, system, or vessel, you know its layout, its weak point, and one secret — which the GM must invent if they haven't. |
| **The Good Stuff** | Your personal kit is masterwork: each venture, choose one extra protection tag or one extra descriptive tag for it. |
| **Triage** | Once per scene, treat a person or machine: clear 1 strain, or downgrade one condition (hard → normal, scene → momentary). |
| **Peer Review** | Your aid on Mental contests doesn't count against the one-Aid limit — two experts genuinely are better than one. |
| **Prototype** | During downtime, build a signature invention, formula, or working as a personal asset with one tag beyond your genre's norm. GM approves it; rivals will want it. |

## Callings

### The Artificer — *makes the future*

Your workshop is where the setting changes.

**Signature — Works of Wonder:** project clocks you personally lead tick on *any* win, not just strong wins. And once per campaign arc, you may propose a wonder that bends the setting's rules — an airship, a vaccine, an ansible — as a faction project clock.

### The Sage — *already read about it*

Somewhere in the library, the archive, or the old songs, this exact problem has happened before.

**Signature — I've Read About This:** once per session, ask the GM for the complete truth about one mystery your studies could plausibly cover. The answer is true and whole.

### The Operator — *one with the machine*

Pick your system at creation — vehicles, networks, bodies, wards. Inside it, you don't think; you flow.

**Signature — In the Zone:** while operating your chosen system, once per scene you may reroll one of your dice for free.
