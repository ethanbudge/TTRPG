# The Heart

*Factions don't die when they lose battles. They die when the people stop showing up. You are why they show up.*

The Heart is the office of **morale and cohesion**: the chaplain, medic, counselor, bard, cook, den mother, or ship's soul. Every organization runs on something no ledger tracks — the Heart tends it. In play, the Heart is the recovery engine and the class that makes the faction's *people* feel real.

## Core Feature — Keep the Flame

Once per scene, tend to an ally — a word, a drink, a prayer, a look across the smoke. Clear 1 strain from them, or clear one morale or social condition (*Shaken, Routed, Demoralized*).

## Faction Synergy

**Quell** and **Tend** actions (calming unrest, healing nodes, clearing faction conditions) gain an edge when a Heart helps run them. If the faction has more than one Heart, it also gains +1 internal Emotional stock (*Morale*) at the Yield step.

## Class Features (start with two)

| Feature | Effect |
| --- | --- |
| **We Eat Together** | Once per faction turn, cover one node's unpaid internal Emotional or Conviction need with pure humanity — no stock spent, no condition taken. Say what you did. (Not the same node twice running; love is not a supply chain.) |
| **Confessor** | People tell you things. Gain an edge reading true feelings and motives, and once per session an NPC confides something the GM must make useful. |
| **Get Up** | Once per session, when an ally is taken out or accepts a scar, sit with them: they return to the conflict with 2 strain cleared, and you narrate why they got back up. |
| **Binding Oath** | Officiate a pact between two characters. While both keep it, each gains an edge on contests directly serving the pact; whoever breaks it takes a lasting condition. |
| **Homefront** | The faction's people love you. At any friendly node, once per scene, establish a small kindness as fact — shelter, a meal, a lookout, a closed mouth. |
| **Rousing Word** | Once per scene, speak to what an ally believes before they roll: they gain an edge on a contest that serves the faction's cause. This isn't Aid — you share no stakes; you just believe in them louder than they doubt themselves. |

## Callings

### The Mender — *bodies*

Field surgeon, herbalist, med-bay chief.

**Signature — Miracle Worker:** once per downtime, fully restore one character — clear all strain and all conditions (not scars), no contest, no cost. Describe the treatment; it's always slightly more than medicine.

### The Kindler — *fires*

You don't heal the fear. You burn it as fuel.

**Signature — Fire Rises:** your rally goals affect every friendly actor in the scene by default, and once per session you may end a *Routed* or *Panicked* condition on your entire side instantly, no roll — one image, one shout, one raised standard.

### The Shepherd — *roots*

Communities, congregations, crews — you grow belonging where you stand.

**Signature — Roots:** nodes where you've spent downtime gain a protection tag (refreshes each faction turn), and their people hide, feed, and defend faction members without being asked.
