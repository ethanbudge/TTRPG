# The Hand

*Everything the faction wants, someone else is holding. You are how it gets taken — and how it gets kept.*

The Hand is the office of **force and protection**: the champion, enforcer, marine, duelist, bodyguard, gunslinger, or siege engineer. Every faction needs one, even the gentle ones — especially the gentle ones. The Hand is the only class allowed to bend the "small numbers" rule, because absorbing punishment *is* the job.

## Core Feature — Interpose

Whenever an ally within Contact or Close range would mark strain, you may take it instead. No limit, no roll — just say you're there. (You can't interpose while *Pinned* or otherwise unable to move.)

## Faction Synergy

**Strike** actions and **node defense** contests gain an edge when a Hand helps run them. If the faction has more than one Hand, it also gains +1 external Physical stock (*Force*) at the Yield step.

## Class Features (start with two)

| Feature | Effect |
| --- | --- |
| **Bulwark** | Your strain track has a sixth box — the only bigger number in the game. |
| **Terror of the Field** | When you take someone out, impose *Shaken* on one witness of your choice (or on a whole unit, at faction scale). |
| **Breaching Action** | Gain an edge when your goal is to create an opening for allies — the door, the line, the shield wall. On a win, the first ally through the opening gains an edge too. |
| **Weapons of the Age** | You are fluent in every armament of your genre. Once per scene, declare an improvised weapon into your hands or re-tag your kit (swap range or descriptive tags to fit the fiction). |
| **Last One Standing** | While you are the only PC still standing in a conflict, gain an edge on everything and refresh all your protection tags. |
| **Measured Violence** | You always choose what "taken out" means for targets you take out — capture instead of kill, humiliate instead of maim — even when the stakes said otherwise. Gain an edge when your goal is explicitly nonlethal. |

## Callings

### The Champion — *one against one*

Single combat is the oldest theater, and you are its star.

**Signature — Single Combat:** once per session, call out a named foe. While the two of you fight each other, you gain an edge against them, and they suffer a setback acting against anyone but you.

### The Warden — *the shield*

You are the wall between the faction and the world.

**Signature — Aegis:** you carry two extra protection tags (refresh each scene), and you may spend your protection tags to negate strain for anyone within Contact range.

### The Breaker — *against the wall itself*

Sieges, demolitions, boarding torches. Structures are just slow opponents.

**Signature — Collateral Approval:** your strong wins against structures, vehicles, formations, or terrain also inflict a condition of your choice on them. At faction scale, once per engagement, a Strike you lead treats the target node's fortification edges as absent.
