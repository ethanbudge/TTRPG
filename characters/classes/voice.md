# The Voice

*Armies take cities. Stories keep them.*

The Voice is the office of **image and persuasion**: the propagandist, diplomat, media star, herald, hype man, court poet, or comms officer. The Voice fights on the battlefield of what people believe is true — and in The Party, that battlefield has the same dice as any other.

## Core Feature — Spin

Once per session, when the faction or an ally suffers a public setback, narrate how you reframe the story. Either gain 1 stock of your faction's external Emotional pool (*Influence* or its campaign equivalent), or negate one reputation-damaging condition before it sticks.

## Faction Synergy

**Campaign** actions (spreading influence, propaganda, diplomacy) gain an edge when a Voice helps run them. If the faction has more than one Voice, it also gains +1 external Emotional stock at the Yield step.

## Class Features (start with two)

| Feature | Effect |
| --- | --- |
| **Silver Tongue** | Gain an edge when negotiating face-to-face with anyone who knows your reputation. |
| **Crowdworker** | Your persuasion goals can target a crowd as easily as a person: one contest, and on a win the whole room swings. |
| **Open Doors** | Once per session, name a social space — a court, a gala, a dockside bar, an encrypted forum. You have an invitation, a contact, or a standing there. Established as fact. |
| **Counterspin** | When a rival faction's propaganda or diplomatic action targets your faction, you may personally contest it: roll your own Emotional in place of the faction's dice. A star can out-shout an empire. |
| **Manufactured Truth** | During downtime, plant a story: win a contest to pin a lasting condition (*Discredited*, *Beloved*, *Feared*) on a person, node, or rival faction. |
| **The Brand** | Allies gain an edge on social contests when they invoke the faction's name anywhere your legend has reached. Extend the legend's reach through Campaign actions. |

## Callings

### The Herald — *speaks to thousands*

Pamphlets, broadcasts, sermons from the steps — you industrialize belief.

**Signature — Saturation:** your faction's Campaign actions reach one extra node or region per action, and once per faction turn you may convert 1 Influence stock into 1 Zeal stock or vice versa — the message becomes fervor, the fervor becomes message.

### The Envoy — *speaks to the one who matters*

Treaties, terms, and truces. You talk to power on power's behalf.

**Signature — Parley:** once per session, force a pause in any conflict — even mid-battle — for one exchange of words under truce: a single social contest. Hostilities resume only if it fails or someone breaks faith, and faith-breakers take a lasting condition (*Oathbreaker*).

### The Idol — *is the message*

The face on the posters. The name on their lips.

**Signature — Adoring Public:** once per session, call on your following for a favor established as fact — a distraction, an alibi, a safehouse, a borrowed car. And while you're present, allies' morale conditions clear automatically at scene's end.
