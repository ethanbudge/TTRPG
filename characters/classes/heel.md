# The Heel

> *"Give me a fast horse and a day's head start and I'll lose an army. Give me two days and I'll bring it back around behind them."*
> — Wren Ashlock, courier of the Free Cantons

The Heel holds the Seat of **movement and reconnaissance**: the scout, ranger, outrider, pilot, navigator, or messenger. When the faction needs eyes on the far ridge, a package across three checkpoints, or a hand at the helm through the debris field, the Heel is already moving. The [Eye](characters/classes/eye.md) decides what's worth knowing; the Heel is the one who goes and finds out.

## Core Feature — The Fast Way

You always know a way through — a shortcut, a gap in the patrol, a route no map shows. Gain an edge on contests to travel, scout ahead, break contact, or carry word and goods through danger. Distance and difficult ground bend for you where they'd stop anyone else.

## Faction Synergy

**Put Down Roots** and actions to scout, expand, or move stock through danger gain an edge when a Heel helps run them. If the faction has more than one Heel, it also gains 1 extra **internal Ideological** stock (*Unity*) at [Income](faction/faction-turn.md#1--income) — the couriers keep the far-flung body feeling like one thing.

## Seat Features (start with two)

| Feature | Effect |
| --- | --- |
| **Tail** | When you shadow someone, roll Physical or Mental against their pool. Win: you learn where they went, who they met, and one secret the GM chooses. Loss: you lose them — or they let you follow. |
| **Vanish** | Edge on goals to slip away, break contact, or disappear in plain sight. You have never once been walked to a door. |
| **Lockstep** | Bring one ally through your infiltrations and crossings: your stealth and route results cover both of you. |
| **Outrider** | When you range ahead of the group before an encounter, roll Mental or Physical against 3. Win: the party gains a primed edge and you name one thing waiting for them. Loss: you learn less, and you may have been seen. |
| **Courier's Word** | Once per session, get a message or a small package to anyone the faction can plausibly reach, across any lines — a warning, a signal, a key — established as fact. |
| **Light Feet** | Edge on contests of speed, balance, and acrobatics: rooftops, rigging, ledges, moving vehicles. Where others climb, you're already across. |

## Callings

### The Shadow — *goes where nothing goes*

Locked doors and sealed decks are suggestions.

**Signature — Ghost Protocol:** once per session, when a barrier or hard condition blocks your movement or perception — the lockdown, the tractor field, the sealed vault — roll Mental or Physical against 4. Win: you're past it, and you narrate how. Loss: it holds, and something notices the attempt.

### The Pilot — *one with the machine*

Choose your craft at creation: a ship, a flyer, a mount, a war-rig, a getaway car.

**Signature — Redline:** while you operate your chosen craft, gain an edge on contests of maneuver, pursuit, and evasion. Once per conflict, push it past what it should bear — reroll one of your dice; if that contest still loses, the craft takes 1 strain or a fitting condition.

### The Ranger — *reads the ground*

Tracker, pathfinder, hunter of the far country.

**Signature — Lay of the Land:** in any terrain you've had time to range through, you and your allies ignore terrain snags and cannot be ambushed. Once per session, reveal a feature of the ground no one else knew — a ford, a bolt-hole, a blind the enemy forgot to watch.
